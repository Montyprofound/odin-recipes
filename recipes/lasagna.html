Homemade Lasagna Recipe


Homemade lasagna is a hearty dish made by layering pasta sheets,savoring meat sauce creamy cheese filling and shredded cheese.It is baked to perfection and results to a beautiful golden brown layer of goodness.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Yellow onion
  • Garlic
  • Marinara sauce
  • Egg
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh parsley
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Italian seasoning

Steps of preparation

Steps of preparation
  1. Prepare the noodles
  2. Cook the meat sauce
  3. Mix the cheese filling
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake
  6. Rest and serve
